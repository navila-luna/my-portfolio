The movie Hidden Figures told the story of three women that defied the odds by creating a series of coded algorithms that played an essential role in launching a man into orbit. Knowing that it happened in real life gave me the courage to learn Java in my sophomore year of high school. Here, I was exposed to the creativity and possibilities that came with coding. My interest and past research on mental health among adolescents led to my passion in designing a website that would help empower individuals to track their daily mental health habits.
